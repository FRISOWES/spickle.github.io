About Spickle

Our Mission

Spickle is on a mission to revolutionize the way you cook and explore recipes. We're bringing the essence of beloved cookbooks to the mobile age, with a focus on empowering chefs and fostering a thriving culinary community.

What We Do

  • **Reinvigorate Cookbooks:** We digitize and index cookbooks from renowned chefs, making them accessible on your mobile device.
  • **Empower Chefs:** We provide chefs with a platform to build their audience and generate income.
  • **Revolutionize Recipe Discovery:** With personalized recommendations based on dietary needs and preferences, finding the perfect recipe is easier than ever.
  • **FlavorDNA Technology:** Our unique FlavorDNA profile captures your taste preferences, allowing for a truly personalized user experience.

Problems

People are complex and there are major shifts in food trends & consumer preferences. Consumers increasingly prioritize health and home cooking, seeking a wide range of recipes that cater to their unique food preferences and dietary restrictions.

High variety in food preferences and scattered recipe sources make it difficult for consumers to efficiently discover recipes that suit their needs, leading to a time-consuming and overwhelming search process.

Spickle curates an extensive digital cookbook library with a diverse collection of best-selling chefs across the globe and trusted cookbooks, enriched with 100+ data points. Our smart cookbook library, The Flavor DNA, learns from each interaction to provide personalized recipe recommendations for you and your dining partners.

The global market for online food delivery is expanding rapidly, with projections showing significant growth in grocery delivery and meal kit delivery. Spickle is positioned to capture a share of this growing market by providing integrated solutions that streamline the cooking and dining experience.

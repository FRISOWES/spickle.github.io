For Chefs

Spickle provides new ways for chefs to monetize, protect content, and reach a wider audience. With non-exclusive licensing, brand & influencer partnerships, and smart upload tools, chefs can focus on creating while Spickle handles the business.

Challenges: Limited opportunities for monetization, lack of direct connection with the audience, difficulty in protecting intellectual property.

Solutions: The Spickle Creator Fund, non-exclusive licensing, brand & influencer partnerships, cookbooks seasoned with digital spice, surf the marketing wave.

For Home-cooks

Discover a smart cookbook library, personalized recommendations, and a community platform to enhance your culinary journey. With the Flavor DNA, Discover Weekly, and The Blender, Spickle tailors recipes to your tastes and preferences.

Challenges: Growing demand for healthy home-cooking, overwhelming and time-consuming search, high variety in food preferences.

Solutions: Smart cookbook library, The Flavor DNA, Spickle Dinner Club, Discover Weekly, The Blender.

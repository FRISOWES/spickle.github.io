Revolutionizing Culinary Experiences

Welcome to Spickle, where culinary traditions meet innovative technology. Our mission is to breathe new life into classic cookbooks by digitizing, translating, and indexing them for a global audience, preserving their original essence while making them accessible and engaging. Imagine what Spotify did for music; Spickle does for recipes and cookbooks.

For Home Cooks and Food Enthusiasts

Tired of the endless search for recipes that fit your dietary needs and taste preferences? Spickle’s FlavorDNA technology curates personalized, high-quality recipes from celebrated chefs worldwide. Whether you're planning a family dinner or a gathering with friends, Spickle makes it easy to find recipes that cater to everyone’s needs.

  • Discover Your FlavorDNA: Our app learns from your interactions to build a unique profile of your dietary preferences, restrictions, and cooking habits, offering tailored recipe recommendations.
  • FlavorBlend: Combine profiles with up to six people to find the perfect recipes for group dining, considering everyone's tastes and dietary needs.
  • Interactive Features: Discover new recipes, organize dinner parties, and even order groceries directly from your local supermarket.

For Chefs and Culinary Creators

Spickle is a dynamic space for chefs to showcase their culinary art, engage with a global audience, and earn revenue. We honor the legacies and rights of chefs by providing them with tools to digitize their cookbooks and reach a wider audience.

  • Digitize Your Cookbook: Our secure upload tools process and enrich your recipes with data, preserving authenticity. No upfront payments—Spickle subsidizes digitization costs.
  • Earn Revenue: Chefs earn through clicks and views on their content, supported by Spickle’s Creator Fund, which reserves a share of net income for royalty payouts.
  • Build Your Brand: Collaborate with brands and sponsors, and gain access to analytics to make informed decisions about your content.
